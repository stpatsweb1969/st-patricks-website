## Proposed Modern Organization

### Top-Level Navigation Structure:
1. **Home** - Hero, quick cards, latest news
2. **Mass & Sacraments** (dropdown)
   - Mass Times & Confessions
   - Baptism (info + forms)
   - First Communion (info + forms)
   - Confirmation (info + forms)
   - Marriage (info + forms)
   - Funerals (info + forms)
3. **Faith Formation** (dropdown)
   - CCD Overview & Registration
   - CCD Calendar
   - Confirmation Prep
   - RCIA / Becoming Catholic
4. **Parish Life** (dropdown)
   - News & Events
   - Parish Events Calendar
   - CYO Basketball
   - Teen Life
   - Ministries & Devotions
   - Volunteer
5. **Bulletins**
6. **Giving**
7. **Contact**

### Key Design Principles:
1. **Forms Center** - One consolidated page where ALL downloadable forms are organized by category
2. **Sacraments Hub** - Each sacrament gets a beautiful page with info, requirements, and relevant forms
3. **Calendar Integration** - Parish Events calendar embedded prominently
4. **Teen Life** - Dedicated youth section with Google Form embed
5. **Admin-managed documents** - Upload PDFs through admin panel, they appear on the right pages automatically
